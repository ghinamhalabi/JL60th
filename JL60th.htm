Scientific Rationale

This meeting celebrates the life and achievements of Prof. John C. Lattanzio. John has made a succession of important contributions to the theory of stellar evolution over a sustained career that has seen him move to Canada, the USA and the United Kingdom before returning to his Australian roots. He remains instantly recognisable on the international stage where he is a speaker of choice on many aspects of stellar evolution and nucleosynthesis. Lattanzio's contributions to research training at all levels are numerous. His undergraduate lectures are delivered with an enthusiasm that has ignited many a career in astrophysics and he has trained a number of excellent graduate students and postdoctoral researchers who have gone on to hold positions worldwide. John is the active president of the International Astronomical Union's Division G3 on stellar evolution. This meeting will cover the topics on which John has had such an impact through his varied and distinguished career.
This meeting is being held to celebrate the scientific career of John Lattanzio. If you wish to contribute photographs of John, you can upload them here.
