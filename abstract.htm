With One Hand Waving Free


29th October - 4th November 2017, Port Douglas, Queensland, Australia

Registration

Online payment for Registration is open

Registration:

The button below will take you to the online registration form and allows you to make payment to register for the conference. Please note that online payments made by ... will not automatically remember the correct amount required from the online registration form. This will need to be entered manually.

Online Registration Form

Early-bird Fee: $500

The conference will be opened at the drinks reception on the evening on Sunday 29th of October and will run until Friday evening 3rd of November 2017. The registration fee covers the attendance documentation, a lunch and refreshment breaks on each day (except Wednesday) and the conference dinner.

Late registration Fee: $750

Student Registration Fee: $250

For postgraduate research students, a discounted registration fee is available.

Extra Persons:

Guests and partners of those who have paid the registration fee are also welcome to attend the Sunday drinks reception, the conference dinner or the Wednesday excursion. An addtional fee for extra persons is listed on the online registration form.

Daily-Rate Particpation:

A daily rate is available for those who are only able to attend for one or two days, and do not wish to pay the full conference registration fee. These are listed on the online registration form.
